Site Name: Discover Bolivia

Reason for Selection: This name was chosen because it directly reflects the site's purpose: inviting national and international tourists to explore and experience Bolivia's wonders. It is clear, memorable, and easy to remember. The name appeals to English-speaking audiences worldwide and projects an inviting, adventurous spirit that matches Bolivia's tourism appeal.

Suggested Domain: discover-bolivia.com

2. Site Purpose

The "Discover Bolivia" website serves as a comprehensive tourism information platform for travelers interested in exploring Bolivia. The site will provide:

  • Detailed information about Bolivia's main tourist destinations (Salar de Uyuni, Lake Titicaca, La Paz, Sucre, etc.)
  • Practical travel guides with recommendations for transportation, accommodation, and restaurants
  • Photo gallery showcasing landscapes and tourist attractions
  • Calendar of festivities and cultural events
  • Contact form for inquiries and additional information requests
  • Useful resources including weather, visa requirements, and safety tips

The goal is to become the top resource for planning trips to Bolivia, increasing tourism and promoting the country's cultural and natural richness.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1:

What is the best time of year to visit the Salar de Uyuni?

This scenario represents tourists planning their trip who need to know optimal visiting times. The site will provide a dedicated section with seasonal information, weather patterns, and the advantages of visiting during dry season (April-October) versus rainy season (November-March) when the salt flats create mirror effects.

Scenario 2:

How can I get from La Paz to Lake Titicaca?

This represents travelers looking for practical transportation information. The site will include detailed transportation guides with bus schedules, private tour options, approximate costs, and travel duration for major routes between tourist destinations.

Scenario 3:

What are the must-see attractions in Sucre?

This scenario reflects tourists researching specific destinations. The site will feature destination pages with top attractions, historical sites, museums, local cuisine recommendations, and suggested itineraries for different trip lengths.

Color Rationale: The green represents Bolivia's lush Amazon forests and valleys, the sandy orange evokes the Salar de Uyuni and high-altitude deserts, the sky blue reflects the clear Andean skies and Lake Titicaca, while the off-white background ensures readability and a clean, modern aesthetic.
